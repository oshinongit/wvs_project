Notes
The idea here is that you can view the world map and get color-based indications of the WVS value. The slider at the bottom can be used to toggle between the different waves of survey data. The darker red a country is, the higher the WVS value See the illustration below. Select the countries that seems interesting by clicking on them in the map, then click on the detail button in the corner to explore them further.
